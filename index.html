Lili Chasioti

In my practice I explore liveness, generativity and interconnectedness within networks. Through installations, site specific work, performances and sound, I create a timeframes and placeholders within which interactions of human with non-human entities and environments take place. These timeframes allow for semi- fictional stories to unfold, using personal experiences as a starting point. Lately, I have become increasingly interested with the systems that are embedded in our homes and personal space, and the kind of relationships and intimacy we develop with them.

** I treated these sketches with a humorous and playful approach, for learning purposes.
